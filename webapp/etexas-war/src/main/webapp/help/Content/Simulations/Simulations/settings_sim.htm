Simulation Settings

Simulations can be configured with detectors, fixed cellular devices, and Road Side Equipment (RSE). Follow the links at right to configure a simulation.

Most settings cannot be changed if there is an existing Execution of the composite that contains the simulation.

Measurements for locations are defined as X, Y, Z coordinates in centimeters (cm), and measured from the center of the intersection in the simulation.
