eTEXAS Help System

Meet eTEXAS

eTEXAS is a platform for Connected Vehicle App simulation. eTEXAS:

  • follows Connected Vehicle standards such as SAE J2735 messages

  • allows Connected Vehicle Apps to send and receive DSRC/WAVE messages, and access data through DSRC units

  • adds a wireless network simulation module to traditional traffic simulation modules
